AI Pulse

Website Planning Document

1. Site Name

AI Pulse

I chose "Pulse" because it suggests something that updates constantly, like a heartbeat. The AI space moves fast — new models, new tools, new drama — and the site is meant to keep up with that pace. The name is short, easy to say, and works as a domain name too. Optional domain: aipulse.io

2. Site Purpose

AI Pulse is a news site focused entirely on artificial intelligence. There are way too many AI announcements happening every week and it's hard to keep track of what actually matters. The goal of this site is to collect the most relevant stories — model releases, research papers, company news, and policy updates — and present them in a clean, organized way so visitors can catch up quickly without having to visit ten different sites.

Each story will include a short plain-English summary so readers without a technical background can still understand why it matters.

3. Scenarios

Questions a typical visitor might come to the site to answer:

  • "What were the biggest AI announcements this week? I've been busy and missed everything."
  • "I keep hearing about a new model — what does it actually do differently from the last one?"
  • "Are there any new AI tools or APIs I should know about as a developer?"
