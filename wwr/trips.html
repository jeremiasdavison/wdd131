Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty: Class IV–V

Duration: Full Day (8 hrs)

Price: $149 per person

Challenge yourself on one of the most thrilling stretches of white water in the region. Powerful currents, tight chutes, and breathtaking canyon scenery make this the ultimate rafting experience for advanced paddlers seeking maximum adrenaline.

Half Day Adventure

Difficulty: Class II–III

Duration: Half Day (4 hrs)

Price: $79 per person

Perfect for families and first-time rafters! Enjoy moderate rapids through stunning landscapes with expert guides who ensure a safe and exciting experience. A great introduction to the world of white water rafting for all ages.

Sunset Run

Difficulty: Class I–II

Duration: Evening (3 hrs)

Price: $59 per person

End your day on the water as the sun dips below the canyon walls. Gentle rapids and golden light make this a magical experience for couples and casual adventurers alike. No prior rafting experience required — just show up and enjoy the ride.
